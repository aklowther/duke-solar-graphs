The information in the first graph comes directly from my Duke Energy bill.


In this graph:
  • "Consumed From Grid" shows the number of kW(h) I used from Duke
    • I do not have a battery, so I rely on the grid when the sun isn't out
  • "Sent to Grid" shows the energy I created in excess of what was being used
  • "kWh Balance" is the total excess I've sent to Duke, providing me a kWh credit
